The Best Caribbean All-Inclusive Resorts for 2026

By Leshard Clarke · 7 min read

Caribbean all-inclusive resorts have evolved way beyond the buffet-and-swim-up-bar formula. Today's best properties rival luxury boutique hotels, with private plunge pools, premium spirits, adults-only zones, and dining that competes with standalone restaurants. Here's my current shortlist for 2026.

1. Sandals Royal Curaçao (Curaçao)

Curaçao is having a moment, and Sandals' newest property is a big reason why. Overwater bungalows, five-star gourmet dining across a dozen restaurants, and the famed "Island Inclusive" credit that gets you experiences off-property. Perfect for couples celebrating something big.

2. Excellence Oyster Bay (Jamaica)

Adults-only, on a private peninsula in Falmouth. Every room is a suite, half of them come with plunge pools, and the food is genuinely excellent. Great value for the quality, and much quieter than the Montego Bay strip.

3. Zoëtry Curaçao Resort & Spa (Curaçao)

A quieter, more boutique all-inclusive experience for travelers who want luxury without the mega-resort crowds. Endless-inclusive concept (yes, top-shelf brands and 24-hour room service), unlimited spa treatments, and a stunning cliffside setting.

4. Secrets Cap Cana (Punta Cana)

Adults-only in the calmer Cap Cana area, away from the party scene. Marble bathrooms, oversized balconies, and one of the best sushi restaurants I've been to at any all-inclusive. Ideal for a relaxed romantic escape.

5. Beaches Turks & Caicos (Turks & Caicos)

The gold standard for families. Twelve-mile Grace Bay Beach right at your door, five distinct "villages" with themes ranging from Caribbean to French, a full water park, and kids' clubs organized by age. Yes, it's expensive. It's also the closest thing to a stress-free family vacation you'll find.

6. Iberostar Grand Rose Hall (Jamaica)

All-suite, all-butler, all-adult. Champagne turndown service, a top spa, and a golf course next door. Underrated compared to the Sandals brand, but arguably better food and better service.

Which one is right for you?

The answer depends on the vibe you want, the size of your group, and your budget. Here's how I usually help clients narrow it down:

  • Adults only: Excellence Oyster Bay, Secrets Cap Cana, Iberostar Grand Rose Hall
  • Family friendly: Beaches Turks & Caicos
  • Splurge: Sandals Royal Curaçao, Zoëtry Curaçao
  • Best value luxury: Excellence Oyster Bay
